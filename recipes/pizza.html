Description:

Pizza is a crowd-pleasing choice for busy weeknights and fun weekend dinners alike. But you don't have to rely on restaurants or takeout for top-notch pizza

Ingredients

  • Crust (Store-bought, Boboli, English muffins)
  • Sauces (Pesto, White, Olive & Garlic)
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Heat the oven
  2. Form the dough and dust it with a little four
  3. Put the pizza on the stone
  4. After 5 minutes, check the pizza
  5. Pizza is done when cheese is melted to a medium-to-dark brown
  6. Enjoy!
